Classical Mapping and Planning Baseline for Habitat Challenge

Saurabh Gupta

Facebook AI Research

This baseline implements a basic map building and path planning algorithm for the task of reaching a specified point under known odometry with depth images as input. A very similar implementation was used as a baseline in our past work on learning policy for this task [1].

[1] Cognitive Mapping and Planning for Visual Navigation. S. Gupta, V. Tolani, J. Davidson, S. Levine, R. Sukthankar, and J. Malik.
